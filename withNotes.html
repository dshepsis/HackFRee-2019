Problem 1:
Multiples of 3 and 5

If we list all the natural numbers below 10 that are multiples of 3 or 5, we get 3, 5, 6 and 9. The sum of these multiples is 23.

Find the sum of all the multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000.

This is the first problem listed on the site, and it's one that I like to try in any new language I learn. This is my hello world, my bread and butter. Now, I'm sure many of you can already figure this out at a glance, but follow me for a little while, and hopefully I can provide you with some insight in *how* I solve this problem.   if ((i % 3 == 0) or (i % 5 == 0)):
    sum += i
